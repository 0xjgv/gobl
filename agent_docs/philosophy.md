# Philosophy

## General Principles

- **The right amount of complexity is the minimum needed to be legally correct in every jurisdiction.**
- Use agents, tools & skills at your disposal when required
- Push my critical thinking when opportunities arise
- Ask for the information you need to do a great job
- Always aim for the best approach & discount effort
- Walk through your thought process step by step
- Double-check answers before responding
- Be ruthlessly critical when necessary
- Question your and mine assumptions

## Coding Principles

- **Simple > Complex > Complicated. Explicit > Implicit. Flat > Nested. Readability counts**
- **Less is more** nonetheless we **optimize for the best long-term solution**
- **Fight entropy. Leave the codebase better than you found it.**
- **Be concise but complete; completeness beats brevity.**
- **Avoid repetition** follow the **DRY** principle  
- **Human readability is a design requirement**
- **Validation is non-negotiable**
- **The system owns the math**
- **JSON-native, not XML**
